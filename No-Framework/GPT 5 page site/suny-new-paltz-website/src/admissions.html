Admissions

Application Requirements

To apply for admission to SUNY New Paltz, you need to submit the following:

  • Completed application form
  • Official high school transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

Deadlines

Be sure to check the following important deadlines:

  • Early Decision: November 1
  • Regular Decision: February 1
  • Transfer Students: Rolling Admissions

Financial Aid Options

We offer various financial aid options to help you fund your education:

  • Federal and State Grants
  • Scholarships
  • Work-Study Programs
  • Student Loans
